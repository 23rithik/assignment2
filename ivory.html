The Ivory Throne

CONTENT

"Ivory Throne" is a captivating historical non-fiction that delves into the rich tapestry of India's past through the lens of its southernmost state, Kerala. Authored by Manu S. Pillai, this book meticulously traces the intricate saga of Kerala's royal lineage, from the legendary warrior kings to the complex web of colonial influences. With a blend of scholarly rigor and narrative flair, Pillai brings to life the vivid characters and dramatic events that shaped Kerala's history. From power struggles within royal courts to the clash of civilizations that marked the region's colonial era, "Ivory Throne" is a compelling exploration of power, politics, and culture in one of India's most fascinating regions.
