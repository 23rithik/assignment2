The Goat Life

CONTENT

"The Goat Life" follows Najeeb and Hakim, Malayali immigrants in Saudi Arabia. Mistakenly employed in a remote goat herd, Najeeb endures hardship, witnessing tragedies. With Hakim and a Somali goatherd, they escape during a wedding. However, the journey is perilous, resulting in Hakim's death and the Somali's disappearance. Najeeb finds refuge with a passing Arab and a Malayali restaurateur after his escape. Despite facing imprisonment due to documentation issues, he eventually returns home, his journey a testament to human resilience.
