Harry Potter and the philosophers stone

CONTENT

"Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" introduces readers to Harry Potter, a young boy who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. Rescued from his cruel relatives by Hagrid, he learns of his parents' magical legacy and begins his journey at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and together they uncover the mystery of the Philosopher's Stone, an object sought by the dark wizard Voldemort for its power of immortality. As Harry navigates school life, he learns about his past and confronts the forces of evil, setting the stage for the epic battle between good and evil that defines the series.
